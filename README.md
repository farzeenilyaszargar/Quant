# My Quant Algo

We are going to find Indian Stocks below their intrinsic value, their customer ratings, their overview, all the important statistic and only then and then to buy a stock

### Phases:

#### Fetch A Stock Financials
- Scraping from trusted sites
- yfinance data

#### Do Measurements:
- DCF (intrinsic value)
- CAGR
- Revenue & Profit
- DE Ratio
- Sharpe Ratio (make overall sharpe ratio of >2)
- Do AI testing of MOAT & Reputation
- Customer Perception
- News Report

#### Prepare Portfolio & Stocks & Show Statistics


Then calculate a weighted score for the stock <br><br>
``` w = 0.3 * DCF Score + 0.2 * CAGR Revenue Growth & Profit Score + 0.1 * ROCE + 0.15 * Customer Satisfaction & MOAT Analysis + 0.05 * DII & FII Activity & D/E + 0.1 * Tailwind Sectors (AI, Indian Tech, Defense, Biotech, Green Energy etc.) + 0.1 * Management Quality Score ```



#### Data Standardization:
- All Money Values are in Cr
- JSON format storage


### Finding Stars In The Trough
- High ROCE (>20%)
- Strong moat (brand, network, switching cost)
- Pricing power
- Low debt
- Revenue growth (>20%)
- Expanding Market or Tailwind Sectors
- Founder quality & management quality
